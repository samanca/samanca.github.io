Research interests: Storage Systems, Distributed Systems and Computer Architecture

I enjoy working at the intersection of storage, database, and distributed systems, with a focus on leveraging hardware primitives and emerging technologies.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from UC San Diego. My research was focused on providing programming support for the emerging non-volatile memory technologies, such as Intel Optane DC Persistent Memory.
